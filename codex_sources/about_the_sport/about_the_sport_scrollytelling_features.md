# Scrollytelling Concepts for "About the Sport"

## 1. BASE Acronym Hero

### Concept

Introduce the acronym BASE through a progressive visual sequence.

### Scroll Behaviour

Each letter appears as the user scrolls:

```text
B
Building
```

↓

```text
A
Antenna
```

↓

```text
S
Span
```

↓

```text
E
Earth
```

Each section is paired with a full-screen image or illustration.

### Purpose

* Instantly communicates the meaning of BASE
* Works internationally
* Creates a strong visual introduction
* Establishes the documentary tone immediately

---

```text

│
│

│
│

300m
```

The camera gradually zooms from skydiving altitude down to BASE altitude.

### Purpose

* More impactful than text alone
* Strong educational value

```text

```


## 8. Disciplines Gallery

### Concept

Horizontal scrolling gallery introducing different disciplines.

### Cards

🪂 Traditional BASE

🏃 Tracking

🦇 Wingsuit

🔄 Acrobatics

🌉 Low Object

👥 Multi Way

### Content per Card

* Large image
* Short introduction
* Key characteristics
* Optional statistics

### Purpose

* Shows diversity within the sport
* Prevents visitors from equating BASE solely with wingsuits
* Creates a highly visual section

### Concept

Visualise how preparation influences outcomes.

### Scroll Behaviour

```text
Preparation
↓
Decision
↓

↓
```
